Your bot already talks. Teach it when to act.

Prism Signals scores every chatbot turn for intent, confidence, emotion, decision stage, topic, and safety, then recommends what to do next. Built for teams that already shipped an LLM, including customer support and in-product assistants. Partner offers are optional when the moment is right.

How Prism Signals works

  1. Send the last few turns — call POST /api/signals/score with recent messages using your SDK key.
  2. Get signals and an action — heuristics always run; optional LLM upgrade per bot for hybrid scoring.
  3. Wire the action into your stack — clarify, retrieve, escalate, change tone, recommend, or optionally show an offer.

Who Signals is for

Customer support bots, in-product assistants, and coaching or lifestyle bots that already run LLMs and need turn-level judgment.

Optional marketplace

When the recommended action is offer and you opt in, Prism can return a contextual partner suggestion. Publishers can also monetize conversations through the existing ad marketplace.

Frequently asked questions about Prism Signals

What is Prism Signals?

A turn-scoring API for LLM apps that returns conversation signals and a recommended next action.

Do I have to show ads?

No. Ads are optional. Judgment is the primary product.

Which stacks are supported?

Any stack that can make an HTTPS request, including OpenAI Assistants, LangChain, Rasa, and custom chat servers.
