# BotGrid - AI Ad Network

## The Business Idea

**BotGrid is an ad network specifically designed for AI chatbots and conversational AI.**

---

## The Problem

The digital advertising industry is worth $500+ billion annually, but it's built entirely for traditional websites and apps. 

Meanwhile, AI chatbots are exploding in popularity:
- ChatGPT has 180+ million users
- Every company is adding AI assistants
- Millions of people use AI daily

**But there's no way to monetize AI conversations!**

---

## Our Solution

BotGrid creates a NEW advertising channel:

```
Traditional Ads:  Website → Display Ads → Users
BotGrid Ads:      Chatbot → AI Ads → Users in conversations
```

### How It Works

**For Publishers (Bot Owners):**
1. Install BotGrid SDK in your chatbot
2. Ads automatically appear in conversations
3. Get paid for every impression and click

**For Advertisers:**
1. Target users IN conversations (higher engagement)
2. Reach users when they're actively interacting with AI
3. Better CTR than traditional display ads

---

## Revenue Model

| Revenue Stream | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| **CPM** | Cost per 1,000 ad impressions |
| **CPC** | Cost per click |
| **CPA** | Cost per action (signup, purchase) |

| Traditional Ads | BotGrid Ads |

## Why This Works

1. **Higher Engagement** - Users actively talking to AI = more receptive
2. **Native Format** - Ads feel part of conversation
3. **First Mover** - No dedicated AI ad network exists
4. **Scalable** - Every new AI bot = new ad inventory
